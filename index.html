Clinical Nutrition & Dietetics

Hospital-based nutrition care, therapeutic diet planning, and patient-centered wellness support.

About

Samreen Ashraf is a Nutrition Science graduate (UMT Lahore, 2025) with strong academic and hospital-based clinical training in dietetics, therapeutic nutrition, and patient care.

Experience

Clinical practice at Indus Hospital, Jinnah Hospital, Punjab Cardiology PIC, Social Security Hospital, University Medical Center, and Surraya Azeem Trust Hospital.

Clinical Services

Disease-Specific Nutrition Planning
Personalized therapeutic diet plans designed to support the management of medical conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, digestive disorders, and food intolerances. Each plan is evidence-based and tailored to your health needs, lifestyle, and goals.

Heart & Metabolic Care
Specialized nutrition strategies focused on improving heart health, managing cholesterol, blood pressure, and metabolic conditions. These plans promote long-term cardiovascular wellness through balanced, sustainable eating habits.

Pregnancy & Postnatal Care
Comprehensive nutrition support for women during pregnancy and after childbirth. This service ensures optimal nutrient intake for maternal health, fetal development, recovery, and breastfeeding support.

Teaching & Counseling
One-on-one nutrition education and counseling sessions designed to build knowledge, encourage healthy choices, and support behavior change. Clients gain practical skills to maintain a balanced diet and long-term well-being.

Blog

Explore expert insights on how proper nutrition supports healing, strengthens immunity, and aids in the management of acute and chronic diseases. This blog shares evidence-based guidance, practical tips, and the latest nutrition research to help you make informed health decisions.
